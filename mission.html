Who We Are

Built for the community.

The E1E Club started with a simple belief — no one in our community should go hungry. We built a nonprofit sandwich shop around that belief and took it to the streets of LA County.

We operate at certified farmers markets every week. But we are more than food. We are a community platform — part sandwich shop, part media outlet, part life-change engine.

A percentage of every sandwich sold goes directly into the E1E Recipient Fund — supporting one real person at a time through housing, education, transportation, and job placement. We document every journey publicly so the community can watch it happen.
